Rising from the rolling hills of the Popolis Despotate, Calveris is a city of labyrinthine streets and an endless hum of intellectual pursuit. The name of Calveris was derived from "calculus" (logic/math) and "veritas" (truth). It is a place where knowledge reigns supreme—a city of scholars, mages, inventors, and bureaucrats, all vying to push the boundaries of what is known. The air is thick with the scent of parchment, ink, and alchemical experiments, while the streets are alive with the murmur of debate, the scratching of quills, and the occasional arcane misfire.

At its peak stands the The Argent Collegium, a sprawling institution of learning and governance, where the greatest minds of the region gather to study, lecture, and scheme. The city's districts are divided by specialised fields of study, with entire neighbourhoods dedicated to philosophy, arcane sciences, engineering, and history. Hidden alleys host forbidden discussions, underground presses publish unsanctioned theories, and in the backrooms of places unknown to the masses, those who see the cracks in the system gather to whisper about what comes next.

## Districts
### [[Darkgate]]
Tucked away in the southwestern corner of Calveris, Darkgate is a district shrouded in secrecy and shadow. The scholars here pursue the dark arcane arts with a fervour bordering on obsession, delving into rituals and experiments that others deem too dangerous or unethical. As a result, much of Darkgate is bisected by towering city walls, forming a labyrinth of secured enclaves where research can be conducted beyond the gaze of the uninitiated. The air hums with residual magic, and flickering blue-green sigils ward doorways and alleys, warning outsiders to proceed at their own peril.

### [[Exilium]]
To the northeast of Calveris lies Exilium, a district of resentment and exile, where the city’s undesirables are left to fend for themselves. Magic is the lifeblood of Calveris, but here, those without arcane talent or magical afflictions are pushed to the fringes, cast aside like discarded experiments. The streets are slick with toxic runoff, viscous puddles that bubble and swirl with unknown alchemical waste. Crumbling tenements lean against each other, their foundations eroded by the very magic that fuels the rest of the city. Crime festers in the alleys, and whispered deals are made in the smoky backrooms of grimy taverns. To walk these streets without purpose is to invite misfortune—or worse.

### [[Greybark Road]]
No street in Calveris pulses with life quite like Greybark Road. Renowned across the continent, this sprawling avenue is a mecca for all things arcane, where merchants and artificers peddle their wares in a cacophony of flashing enchantments and clanking automata. The air is thick with the scent of burning incense and arcane reagents, and the streets are perpetually crowded with seekers of magical power. Stalls overflow with floating grimoires, bottled storms, and spell-infused trinkets, while artificers proudly display clockwork thopters, steel golems, and arcane contraptions that defy explanation. To the unprepared, Greybark Road is a dizzying maze of flashing sigils, cryptic sales pitches, and half-heard deals whispered behind beaded curtains—but for those with coin and curiosity, there is no better place to uncover the city’s magical secrets.

### [[Luxor]]
At the heart of Calveris lies Luxor, the beating intellectual core of the city. Its pristine sandstone streets gleam under the sun, meticulously cleaned by unseen arcane forces, and its architecture is a testament to the scholarly pursuits that define Calveris. Towering spires and marble academies dominate the skyline, their halls filled with scribes, researchers, and apprentices debating the finer points of magical theory and philosophical paradoxes. Libraries stretch for city blocks, their shelves lined with forbidden tomes and cutting-edge research, while pristine lecture halls echo with heated scholarly disputes. Life here is dictated by the relentless pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and discovery, and those who walk these streets carry themselves with the quiet confidence of those who shape the future.

### [[Noonbridge]]
By all appearances, Noonbridge is the most unremarkable district in Calveris. Its well-paved suburban streets are lined with neatly kept homes, ornate gardens, and pristine lantern-lit walkways. Yet, behind the veneer of normalcy lies true power—this tiny, unassuming district is home to the most influential figures in the region. Within these walls, archmages, council leaders, and the city’s elite reside in cloistered luxury, far removed from the bustling chaos of the scholar’s city. The gates of Noonbridge are heavily guarded, patrolled by a relentless rotation of Calveris’ finest enforcers, who deny entry to all but the most essential visitors. Those who do manage to pass the watchful eyes of the gatekeepers might find themselves rubbing shoulders with figures who hold the fate of the continent in their hands.

### [[Oldgate]]
Perched just outside the south-eastern city walls of Calveris, Oldgate is where tradition and discovery intertwine. Despite the uninspired name, this district is a cornerstone of botanical research, home to vast greenhouses, sprawling herbal gardens, and laboratories dedicated to the study of rare flora. The scent of fresh soil and exotic plants lingers in the air, and winding dirt roads lead out into fertile farmland, stretching over rolling hills as far as the eye can see. The scholars here seek to unravel the mysteries of nature—from alchemical herbs to magically infused crops—and their work supplies both the city and distant lands with potent elixirs and life-saving medicines. Though not as densely populated as other districts, Oldgate is quietly vital to Calveris’ survival.

### [[Omberlyn]]
Omberlyn exists in the periphery of memory, a district seldom spoken of and even less frequently visited. Officially, it is nothing more than an administrative oversight, a tangle of misplaced records and abandoned structures that the Collegium has deemed unworthy of attention. Yet, those who wander its mist-laden streets know better—Omberlyn is a secret buried in plain sight.

Nestled between Greybark Road and Darkgate in Calveris, the district is a labyrinth of forgotten buildings, shuttered storefronts, and alleyways that seem to double back on themselves. Signs of habitation are subtle—a candle flickering behind cracked shutters, a worn path through the dust—but the residents here live as shadows, their existence undocumented, their identities unconfirmed.

The air is thick with the scent of damp parchment and old ink, as if the very walls remember the knowledge erased from history. Faded murals, their meanings long lost, stretch across crumbling facades, depicting constellations that no current star chart recognizes.

Those who seek forbidden knowledge, unregulated research, or those simply looking to disappear eventually find their way to Omberlyn. And once they do, they are faced with an unsettling realization—Omberlyn found them first.
